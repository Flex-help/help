Pre-Setup

  • Make sure you have all the stations you’re able to pick up from selected in your filters. There’s a list of available stations on this map. Note: The map is slightly out of date, but it’s the best resource currently available online. Credit to Rodritari on Reddit for the map.
  • Set the times you can actually arrive at all the sites you’ve marked as active in your filters.

What to do

    Unless you’re in a low driver availability area, shifts are extremely hard to get. You can sometimes get reserved shifts, but these are not filtered to your preferred routes or times, so they’re rarely useful. Feel free to use my “spam method” below to improve your chances.

  1. Place two fingers on the screen in the following positions and tap them rapidly, one after the other. This allows you to refresh, select a block, and accept it in a continuous cycle.
  2. Spam until you get a green “Offer scheduled” notification. DO NOT stop to read the shift details before pressing accept — if you pause to check, the shift will already be gone.
  3. Open your calendar and sort your shifts. If you don’t want a shift you’ve just accepted, forfeit it and keep searching. You can forfeit within 5 minutes of accepting without any consequences.
    For example:
    if you accidentally accept a shift that starts in 10 minutes, you can safely forfeit it as long as you do so within that 5-minute window.
