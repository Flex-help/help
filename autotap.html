AutoTap

Automate the process

    This is a paid app costing £5 per month or £25 per year. If you use AutoTap properly, it’s realistic to pull in £400–£500 per week.

  • Use this app to automate getting shifts so you’re not constantly tapping your screen.
  • Other apps and services often charge a commission on each shift you book — and that adds up fast. With AutoTap, you just pay a one-time fee and can book an unlimited number of shifts.

Pre-Setup

  • Make sure you have all the stations you’re able to pick up from selected in your filters. There’s a list of available stations on this map. Note: The map is slightly out of date, but it’s the best resource currently available online. Credit to Rodritari on Reddit for the map.
  • Set the times you can actually arrive at all the sites you’ve marked as active in your filters.
